Dumps the Context-free grammar in BNF format.

Iterates over the production rules defined in the configuration and prints them in a human-readable BNF format. (e.g.A ::= B | C)

Parameters
outOutput file stream
cfgUnderlying CFG
tk_namesThe token name or literal representation (e.g., IDENT or +). If you do not want to put double quotes around token name, put @ at the beginning of the name.
nt_namesThe raw name of the nonterminal (e.g., expr). The dumper handles the surrounding < and > characters automatically.
